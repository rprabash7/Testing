Product Description

Immerse yourself in the timeless elegance of our exquisite Banarasi Silk Saree in Royal Red. This masterpiece showcases the legendary craftsmanship of Varanasi weavers, featuring intricate golden zari work that creates mesmerizing patterns across the rich silk fabric.

The saree's lustrous texture and stunning drape make it perfect for weddings, festivals, and special celebrations. The deep royal red color symbolizes prosperity and grace, while the traditional motifs woven with pure zari threads add a regal touch to your appearance.

Key Features:

  • Handwoven by master artisans from Varanasi
  • Pure Banarasi silk fabric with natural sheen
  • Intricate golden zari work in traditional patterns
  • Rich pallu with elaborate designs
  • Comes with matching blouse piece
  • Perfect for weddings, festivals, and special occasions

Styling Suggestions:

Pair this magnificent saree with traditional gold jewelry, statement earrings, and an elegant clutch. For a contemporary look, style it with a designer blouse featuring modern cuts or embellishments. The versatile color complements both traditional and fusion styling.

Care Instructions

Dry Clean Only

Professional dry cleaning recommended to maintain fabric quality

Do Not Bleach

Avoid using bleach or harsh chemicals

Avoid Direct Sunlight

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight

Iron on Low Heat

Use low heat setting and iron from reverse side
